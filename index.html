Privacy Policy

We don't collect any personal data.
Feel free and safe to use NetPulse. Your personal information stays on your device — always. The app may show ads to support free access.

01 — Data Collection

What We Don't Collect

NetPulse is built on a simple principle: your data belongs to you. We collect absolutely nothing.

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data is stored or transmitted
  • No usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • No network data sent to external servers
  • No third-party advertising or analytics SDKs
  • No account registration required
  • No cookies or cross-app tracking
02 — How The App Works

What NetPulse Does

📡
Network Reading
NetPulse reads your WiFi name, signal strength, and speed purely to display it on your screen. This data is never stored or transmitted anywhere.
📍
Location Permission
Android requires location permission to read your WiFi network name (SSID). We use this solely to display your network name — nothing else. No location coordinates are ever accessed or stored.
⚙️
Settings Storage
Your app preferences (toggles, DNS selection) are saved locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device.
🛡️
DNS Optimization
When you switch DNS to Cloudflare (1.1.1.1) or another provider, your DNS queries go directly to that provider — not through us. We have no visibility into your DNS activity.
03 — App Permissions

Why We Need Permissions

NetPulse requests only the permissions strictly necessary for its features:

  • INTERNET — to measure ping latency to DNS servers
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE — to read WiFi signal strength and connection info
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — required by Android to read WiFi network name only
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect connection type (WiFi / Mobile Data)
  • KILL_BACKGROUND_PROCESSES — to throttle background apps during boost
  • WAKE_LOCK — to keep optimization running while screen is on
04 — Third Parties

Third-Party Services

NetPulse may display advertisements to support free access to the app. Ad networks may collect certain non-personal, anonymized data (such as device type or general location) for ad targeting purposes. We do not share your personal data with advertisers.

The app also makes the following network requests:

  • Ping requests to 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare) to measure latency — no personal data attached
  • Google Fonts loaded from fonts.googleapis.com for the app interface typography
  • Ad network requests (if ads are shown) — governed by the respective ad network's privacy policy

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

05 — Children's Privacy

Children's Privacy

NetPulse does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
